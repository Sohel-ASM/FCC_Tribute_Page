Donald John Trump

45th President of the USA
Official portrait of Donald J. Trump, 45th president of the USA, at his Oval Office.

    Here's a timeline of President Trump's life:

  • 1946 - Born in New York City, borough of Queens. He is of German and Scottish descendeny from his father and mother's side respectively.
  • 1968 - Graduated with a B.Sc in economics from Wharton School of the University of Pensylvania. While in college, he was deferred four time from armed services due to various health conditions.
  • 1968 - Began his career at the family owned E. Trump & Son real estate development company, which at that time was under the authority of his father, Fred Trump.
  • 1971 - Became president of the E. Trump & Son, and renamed the company to the Trump Organization.
  • 1977 - Married his first wife Czech model Ivana Zelnickoba, with her he bears three children: Donald Jr.(1977), Ivanka(1981), and Eric(1984). Trump and Ivana divorced in 1992.
  • 1980 - Two years earlier bought major share in the derelict Commodore Hotel in Manhattan, after remodelling, reopened it as the Grand Hyatt Hotel.
  • 1983 - Finished developing 58 story Trump Tower, in Manhattan. Established his primary residence and the headquarters of the Trump Organization in it.
  • 1985 - Bought Mar-a-Lago estate in Palm Beach, Florida. He later turned it into a private club.
  • 1993 - Married his second wife actress Marla Maples. With her he bears a daughter name Tiffany. The couple divorced in 1999.
  • 1994 - Opened Trump International Hotel and Tower, a 44-story luxury residential and hotel property.
  • 2005 - Trump married his third wife Slovenian model Melania Knauss. With her he bears a son, Barron. With Trump being president, consequently, Melania become the first lady.
  • 2015 - Announced his bid for 2016 presidential run.
  • 2016 - Entered into 2016 presidential race as a republican candidate.
  • 2017 - Assumed Office of the President of the USA after defeating democrat presidential nominee Hillary Clinton.
  • 2018 - Forbes, in its billionaire ranking estimated Trump's net worth around $3.1 billion, making him one of the richest politician in the history of the USA.
